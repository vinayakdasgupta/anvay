Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

anvay is an open-source application designed for topic modeling and text analysis. It respects your privacy and does not collect, store, or share any personal data by default.

1. Data Processing

All uploaded text files are processed locally within the running server environment. Anvay does not transmit or log your documents to any external servers or third parties.

2. Cookies & Analytics

This site may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Plausible) to track general usage patterns. These tools help us improve the application's functionality and understand how users interact with the interface. No personally identifiable information is collected, and no cookies are used beyond those essential for session functionality.

3. Open Source Notice

Anvay is an open-source project released under the MIT License.

4. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. We encourage users to review it occasionally for updates.
